What is Renters insurance?

Renters insurance is for anyone renting a dorm, apartment, condo, house, etc. and replaces your belongings in case of theft or disaster. A renters policy covers a broad range of losses and disasters including:

  • Theft and Vandalism
  • Extreme Weather
  • Fire Damage
